About Me

I’m a data scientist with a strong foundation in business strategy, specializing in transforming complex data into actionable insights that drive operational efficiency and informed decision-making. With expertise in natural language processing, machine learning, and predictive modeling, I build tools and workflows that help organizations make smarter, faster decisions.

I’m especially passionate about making data accessible and turning raw inputs into clear narratives that empower teams. Outside of work, you’ll find me playing the piano, running trails, practicing yoga, or watching international films.

Core Values and Principles

Accuracy as a Foundation: Good insights come from good data. I focus on clean inputs, and well-documented processes.

Purposeful Questioning: I start with the right questions because, quoting Mark Twain, “Data is like garbage. You’d better know what you are going to do with it before you collect it.”

Analytical Exploration: Data can mislead without proper context. I invest time in EDA because rushed insights rarely lead to robust solutions.

Insightful Storytelling: I believe, as W. Edwards Deming said, "Without data, you’re just another person with an opinion."
